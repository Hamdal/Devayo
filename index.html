About Me

Bio

Hey there, my name is Hameed Ayomide. I'm a programmer from Nigeria, Africa and a student of pure and applied physics from Ladoke Akintola University Of Technology. I've been coding a little over a year now and i've loved every minute of it. I've mostly worked on web design and development, think HTML, CSS, PHP and JavaScript (including some libraries and frameworks), i've also been getting more hands on with android development with Java, cross-platform mobile development with React Native and cross-platform desktop development with electron.

I love working with other programmers to gain more experience whenever i have the time and opportunity. When am not studying or coding, am with friends, watching a movie, playing computer games or roller skating.
